## 💡 **Pro Tips**

1. **Submit during business hours** (9 AM - 5 PM PST) for faster review
2. **Use high-quality screenshots** to stand out
3. **Respond quickly** to reviewer questions
4. **Be active on platforms** (Product Hunt, Hacker News) after submission
5. **Update listings quarterly** with new features
6. **Track which directories** send the most traffic
