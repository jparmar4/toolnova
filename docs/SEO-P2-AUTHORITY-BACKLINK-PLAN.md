# SEO P2 Authority + Backlink Plan (90 Days)

## Goal
Increase domain authority and trust to unlock top-10 rankings for priority tool keywords.

## Link acquisition channels
1. **Programmatic linkable assets**
   - Publish: "Free Tools for Students" resource page
   - Publish: yearly benchmark post (e.g., PDF workflow time savings)
2. **Listicle placements**
   - Reach out to bloggers covering student productivity, AI tools, PDFs
3. **Directory submissions**
   - Product Hunt alternatives, startup directories, AI tool directories
4. **Educational communities**
   - Useful resource mentions in university communities and study forums
5. **Guest contributions**
   - 2/month with contextual dofollow links to category/tool pages

## Monthly targets
- Month 1: 20 referring domains
- Month 2: +25 referring domains
- Month 3: +30 referring domains
- Anchor profile: 70% branded/natural, 20% partial match, 10% exact

## Quality rules
- Prefer topical relevance over DR vanity.
- Avoid spammy PBNs and bulk automated links.
- Keep landing pages high-quality before outreach.

## Support content to publish
- 8 comparison pages (ToolNova vs manual workflow)
- 12 informational blog posts mapped to tool intents
- 6 use-case pages for student + professional segments

## KPI
- Referring domains growth
- Non-brand clicks growth
- Top-20 keyword count
- Top-10 keyword count
