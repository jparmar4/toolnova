## Why this order works

- Drafting first avoids blank-page delay
- Paraphrasing before grammar gives cleaner final output
- Summarizing at the end helps quick revision and recall

---

## Quality checklist before submission

- Thesis statement is clear
- Each paragraph has one core idea
- Sources are cited correctly
- Grammar and punctuation pass completed
- Final read for tone and flow

## FAQ

### Is AI writing safe for assignments?
Use according to your institution policy and always review manually.

### Can I skip manual editing?
Not recommended. AI assists; you finalize.

### Which tool should I start with?
Start with essay or paragraph generation, then refine.

## Final takeaway

Winning workflow = structure → refine → polish. Use AI as a co-pilot, not an autopilot.
