## Why PDF formatting breaks after merge

Most formatting issues happen because of:
- Different source standards (PDF 1.4 vs 1.7, print/export variants)
- Missing or subsetted fonts
- Re-compression of images
- Incorrect page box handling (MediaBox/CropBox)
- Low-quality web converters that rasterize pages

When the merger preserves native page objects instead of flattening everything into images, output quality stays consistent.
